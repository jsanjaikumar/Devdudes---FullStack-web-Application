Shipping & Delivery Policy

At DevDudes, we aim to ensure that your orders are delivered in a timely manner.

Delivery Timeline

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Delivery time typically ranges between 3-7 business days depending on your location.

Serviceable Locations

We currently deliver across India. International delivery is not available at this time.

Shipping Charges

Shipping is free for all prepaid orders. COD (Cash on Delivery), if available, may carry an additional charge.

Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS to monitor your package.

Delays

While we try our best to deliver on time, external factors (weather, courier delays, etc.) may affect delivery schedules. In such cases, we will keep you informed.

Contact Us

For any shipping-related queries, contact us at support@devdudes.tech.
